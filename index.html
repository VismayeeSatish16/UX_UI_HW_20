Humming Bird

Transforming regional public transport with dynamic, real-time route optimization. Reducing travel times, operational costs, and our global carbon footprint.

Problem Statement View Prototype

Problem Statement

In regional areas, both local residents and visitors face challenges with inadequate public transportation options and the absence of Uber services. Our goal is to provide a seamless, time-efficient, and affordable travel experience through our app. How can we enhance public transport in regional areas to attract more customers and increase satisfaction among existing users?

Our Hypothysis User Journey

Hypothesis

Our app aims to revolutionize the transportation experience by providing real-time location-based services to passengers. By allowing passengers to input their current location and desired destination, our app will optimize routes and significantly reduce travel time and expenses. Additionally, our app will enable transportation companies to efficiently map user locations and routes, leading to more streamlined operations. This will result in reduced petrol and service expenses for the companies while making a positive impact on their carbon footprint.

User Centered Design User Feedback

User-Centered Design

Our design process follows the 'I Like, I Wish, and What If' methodology, a user-centered feedback approach. We actively gather insights from users and stakeholders to identify strengths ('I Like'), areas for improvement ('I Wish'), and innovative ideas ('What If'). This iterative process ensures our website delivers an exceptional user experience tailored to their needs.

User Testing & Feedback

User Testing & Feedback

Improvements made after User Testing

  1. Social media login for user convenience and alternative account creation.
  2. Real-time route updates and time-saving information. Trip saving feature for easy access to past and planned journeys.
  3. Clear and intuitive navigation for easy exploration.
  4. Search functionality for discovering points of interest and relevant trip information.
  5. Customization options for user preferences and settings.
  6. Feedback mechanism to encourage user communication and continuous improvement.
